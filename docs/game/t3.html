Bot Profiles

Nice bot always chooses the lowest-numbered positions, so you can easily beat it.

Random bot always chooses whatever it feels like, even if it is a bad choice. If you don’t like its choice, you can undo your move and redo it.

Optimal bot never loses. But it can defeat you.
