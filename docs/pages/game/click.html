--- title: Clicking Game (remake) date: "2019-11-02 17:53:26 -0600" entry: app_game_click ---

After 15 years, I decided to remake my original clicking game, made in 2005 with Windows Forms and VB .NET. Today, it is essentially the same game, but it is a modern web game.

Click the button to gain points, but speed will increase with every click. The challenge is to click it 250 times, or if the difficulty is too high, find the secret cheat.
