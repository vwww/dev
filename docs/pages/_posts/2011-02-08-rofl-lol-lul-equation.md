<mark>Try to solve</mark> this equation:

$$2a - a = a^2 - a^2 + a-2$$

<!--more-->

Let's simplify it first:

1. <mark>Remove</mark> duplicates:

   $$2a - a = a - 2$$

2. <mark>Reduce</mark> like terms by cancelling:

   $$a = a - 2$$

And <mark>it cannot be solved</mark> xD
